Web dictaphone





Press record, read this sentence/word, and then press stop. If you would like a do-over, simply record the clip again. To lock in your recording, press 'confirm'. The system will download your final recording for each stimulus into your downloads folder. When you are done, move these audio files from your downloads folder to a new folder, zip it, and send it to brianleonardconsulting@gmail.com
